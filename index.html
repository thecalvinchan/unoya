Who Are We?

Unoya.

Eunoia (pronounced unoya) comes from the greek word εὔνοια, which means beautiful thinking and a well mind. Unoya was founded under the belief that living with an illness should not hinder or detract from the quality of one's life. Given the recent popularity of crowd-sourcing platforms, we know that social media can be used to support those diagnosed with cancer and help them achieve their dreams.
